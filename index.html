Audition Recording Sessions

Suggested pricing: $65 for 30 minutes or $110 for 60 minutes.

Recording sessions include basic setup, coaching between takes, clean trimming, separating files if needed, and delivery through a digital folder.

Note: Recordings are not professionally altered, spliced, pitch-corrected, or edited in a way that would violate audition rules. The goal is to help students submit a clean and honest recording of their best performance.

About Me

Michael Afu is a Sacramento-based music educator, performer, and founder of Afu Music Studio. He began his musical journey in elementary school before continuing through band programs in middle school and at Antelope High School, where he performed in the Wind Ensemble, the Marching Band, the Jazz Band, and multiple honor ensembles, including the California All-State Honor Band.


He earned his Bachelor of Arts in Music Education and Teaching Credential from California State University, Fresno, where he performed with the Fresno State Symphony Orchestra, Wind Orchestra, Symphonic Band, FOOSA (Fresno Summer Orchestra Academy), and Bulldog Marching Band. In addition to performing with various community bands and orchestras throughout California, he has also had opportunities to perform internationally and at prestigious venues, including performances in Australia and at the Walt Disney Concert Hall in Los Angeles.


He later completed his Master of Music in Music Technology at Southern Utah University. Through Afu Music Studio, Mr. Afu helps students build strong fundamentals, prepare for auditions, and grow into confident, capable musicians.
